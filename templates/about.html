Welcome to Burger King

At Burger King, we pride ourselves on serving high-quality, great-tasting, and affordable food to our customers. Since our humble beginnings in 1954, we have grown to become the second-largest fast food hamburger chain in the world. With more than 18,000 locations in over 100 countries and U.S. territories, we are committed to providing exceptional customer service and delicious meals that keep our guests coming back for more.

Our Story

Burger King was founded in 1954 by James McLamore and David Edgerton in Miami, Florida. The original concept was simple: to offer customers great-tasting food at a reasonable price. The first Burger King restaurant quickly became popular for its flame-grilled burgers, which set us apart from our competitors. Over the years, we have expanded our menu and introduced new items, but our commitment to quality has remained unchanged.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide our customers with food they love, made with high-quality ingredients, and served by friendly and knowledgeable staff. We believe that everyone deserves a great meal, and we strive to make sure every visit to Burger King is a positive experience.

Our Food

At Burger King, we believe in using only the best ingredients to create our signature flame-grilled burgers, crispy fries, and refreshing beverages. Our Whopper, introduced in 1957, remains a customer favorite and a cornerstone of our menu. In addition to our classic offerings, we continuously innovate to bring new and exciting options to our guests, including plant-based alternatives, fresh salads, and tasty desserts.

Commitment to Quality

We take food quality seriously at Burger King. Our beef patties are 100% beef, free from fillers, preservatives, and additives. We source our ingredients from trusted suppliers and ensure that every meal meets our high standards for taste and safety. Our commitment to quality extends beyond our food to our packaging and sustainability practices.

Sustainability and Responsibility

As a global brand, we recognize our responsibility to the environment and the communities we serve. Burger King is committed to reducing our environmental footprint through various initiatives, including sustainable sourcing, energy-efficient practices, and waste reduction programs. We also support local communities through charitable partnerships and employee volunteer programs.

Innovation and Growth

Innovation has always been at the heart of Burger King. From the introduction of the drive-thru service in the 1970s to the launch of our mobile app and delivery service, we continuously seek new ways to enhance the customer experience. Our commitment to growth has also led us to expand into new markets, bringing the Burger King experience to more people around the world.

Join Our Team

At Burger King, our employees are our most valuable asset. We offer a dynamic and inclusive work environment where team members can grow and thrive. Whether you're starting your career or looking for new opportunities, we invite you to join our team and become part of the Burger King family.

Thank You

We appreciate your loyalty and support. Thank you for choosing Burger King and for allowing us to serve you. We look forward to continuing to bring you the delicious food and exceptional service that you love.

Contact Us

We value your feedback and are always here to assist you. If you have any questions, comments, or concerns, please feel free to reach out to us through our customer service channels.
